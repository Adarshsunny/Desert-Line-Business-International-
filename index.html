ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Desert Line Business International LLC is a trusted supplier of construction and building materials. We provide a broad range of high-quality materials to meet the evolving needs of the industry. With a strong commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we support projects of all sizes by delivering timely, efficient, and cost-effective solutions tailored to our clients' requirements.

OUR LEGACY & RANGE

Our building materials shop is a proud member of the Desert Line Business Group, a trusted name in Oman for quality and reliability. We have been a leading supplier of essential building materials, catering to both large construction projects and everyday maintenance needs. Our extensive product range includes top-quality wood, steel, and plywood, as well as essential hardware items such as door locks, hinges, and cables. We also stock a comprehensive selection of plumbing items, pipes, sanitary products, paints, power tools and personal protective equipment (PPE), including gloves, jackets, and safety helmets.

OUR APPROACH

Our skilled team, hands-on approach, and deep industry knowledge allow us to deliver solutions tailored to each client's needs, with a strong emphasis on safety, durability, and design.

YOUR TRUSTED PARTNER

We are your go-to destination for all your construction and industrial needs. Our inventory features top brands like Jotun Paints, Asian Paints, along with Makita, Bosch power tools and Schneider and ABB electrical items. We offer a wide variety of tools, from hand tools to power tools, and specialized plumbing materials, construction machinery, including cement mixers, generators, welding machines, and water pumps. Conveniently located and with a robust delivery service, we ensure prompt delivery across Oman, including the Misfa and Bousher regions.
